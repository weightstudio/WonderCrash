About WeightPlay

WeightPlay is a family-friendly animal browser game platform created by WeightStudio.

Our goal is to build a growing library of original web games that are easy to start, friendly on phones and tablets, and useful for short play sessions at home. WeightPlay is designed for kids, families, and casual players who want playful games with clear age guidance and simple progress feedback.

What We Build

  • Original animal-themed web games that can be played from the browser.
  • Family-friendly games with clear age, topic, gameplay, and skill categories.
  • Animal games, puzzle games, memory games, arcade games, and light educational games.
  • Mobile-first experiences that do not require account registration for basic play.

For Parents

Each playable game is planned with parent-readable information such as recommended age, difficulty, estimated play time, and skills practiced. Games may help children practice skills like memory, focus, reaction, logic, color recognition, hand-eye coordination, problem solving, and animal knowledge through play.

Progress and scores are meant for fun, encouragement, and local progress tracking only. WeightPlay does not provide IQ testing, medical diagnosis, psychological testing, or formal school assessment.

Privacy-Friendly Play

Basic play does not require a login. Some games may store progress, settings, scores, coins, diamonds, or language preferences in the browser using local storage so players can continue on the same device.

Platform Direction

WeightPlay is not a single game. It is a long-term platform that will continue adding new animal-world games, improving existing games, expanding localization, improving accessibility, and building parent-friendly features that help families understand what each game practices.

WeightPlay 是由 WeightStudio 製作的親子友善動物網頁遊戲平台。我們會持續推出適合兒童、親子與休閒玩家的原創遊戲，並重視分齡、能力分類、手機友善、容易開始，以及短時間遊玩的體驗。
